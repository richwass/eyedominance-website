Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

The EyeDominanceApp is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your data belongs to you, and we've built our app to ensure it stays that way.

Data Collection

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers
  • Camera access is used solely for capturing images during eye dominance tests
  • Test images and results are stored locally on your device only
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • We do not track your location or device usage

Camera Usage and Image Storage

  • Camera permission is required for the eye dominance test functionality
  • Images are captured and saved locally on your device for offline use
  • You can access saved images within the app for reference
  • Images remain on your device and are never transmitted to our servers
  • We have no access to your saved images outside of the app's functionality
  • You can delete saved images through the app interface
  • You can revoke camera access anytime in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera

Test Data

  • Eye dominance test results are stored only on your local device
  • Manual point markings (pupil and finger positions) are processed locally
  • Historical test data and images remain on your device for tracking purposes
  • All data can be deleted by deleting the app or through in-app options

App Analytics

  • We may collect anonymous app usage statistics through standard iOS analytics
  • This includes app crashes and basic usage patterns to improve the app
  • No personal identifying information is included in these analytics

Data Security

  • All images and test data are stored locally using standard iOS security
  • Data is protected by your device's passcode/biometric security
  • We cannot access your data remotely

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes. We will notify users of significant changes through app updates.
