Common Eye Dominance Measurement Techniques

Simple Step-by-Step Process

Getting Started

High level instructions for easy and consistent eye dominance measurement

Position Setup

Have your subject stand about 8-10 feet away from you while holding your phone

Right Hand Position

Have them face you directly with their right hand centered on chest, index finger extended

Pointing Technique

Have them look directly at the camera lens and point without looking back at their finger

Take Right Hand Photo

Capture the photo with the right hand pointing steadily at the camera

Repeat for Left Hand

Repeat the exact same steps for the left arm/hand

Mark Points & Analyze

Select pupils and pointing finger location in each image, then click analyze
